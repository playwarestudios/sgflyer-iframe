The Singapore Flyer is a ferris wheel at Marina Bay in Singapore. It is one of the largest ferris wheels in the world, with a height of 165 meters (541 feet) and a diameter of 128 meters (420 feet). The wheel has 28 glass capsules, each with a capacity of 28 people, for a total of 816 riders. The ride takes about 30 minutes, and the wheel completes a full rotation every 25 minutes.
